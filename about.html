  • Define Your Target Audience

    Defining your target audience is a critical step in any digital marketing campaign. By understanding who your ideal customer is, you can create marketing materials that resonate with them and ultimately drive more conversions.

    To define your target audience, you'll want to consider factors such as demographics (age, gender, income, location), psychographics (values, interests, lifestyle), and behaviors (online habits, purchasing behavior). You may also want to conduct market research to gather additional insights.
